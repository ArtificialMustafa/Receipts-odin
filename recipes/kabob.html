Learn how to make delicious Afghan kabobs with this easy recipe! Kabobs have been an exquisite cusine of the middle eastern cultures with variety of countries forming their own variations of it. Juicy chicken breats grilled over charcoal or flame adds a charred taste that is loved by many.

  • Ingredients:
  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. In a large bowl, combine the chicken, onion, bell pepper, olive oil, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to coat the chicken and vegetables.
  2. Thread the chicken and vegetables onto skewers, alternating between the chicken and vegetables.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Grill the kabobs for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has nice grill marks.
  4. Remove the kabobs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your delicious Afghan kabobs!
